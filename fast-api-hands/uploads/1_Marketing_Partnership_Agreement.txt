MARKETING PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
Between: RedBill GmbH ("Vendor") and Retail Partner ("Client")

1. EXCLUSIVITY
The Client agrees to grant RedBill GmbH exclusivity in the "Energy Drink" category. No other energy drink brands (e.g., Monster, Rockstar) shall be sold on premises.
IN EXCHANGE: Vendor grants a flat 10% discount on all orders, superseding standard volume tiers.

2. BRANDING
Vendor will provide:
- 1x Illuminated Window Sign
- 1x Counter Mat
- 2x Branded T-Shirts for staff

3. EVENT SUPPORT
For any local event hosted by Client (max 2 per year), Vendor agrees to provide up to 100 complimentary cans for promotional distribution.
